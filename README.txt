########### NOUGHTS AND CROSSES #############


The game is Noughts and Crosses (Tik Tak Toe if your American) and this is a two player game.
The rules are simple, you and your opponent take it in turns to fill the board with whatever 
marker you are, either Noughts or Crosses. The aim of the game is the make a line of three markers on the 
board before you opponent whilst also stopping them to make a line of three. Good Luck!
